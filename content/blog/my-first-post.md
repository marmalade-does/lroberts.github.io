## How to add a new post

1. Create a new `.md` file in `content/blog/`
2. Add front matter at the top:
   ```yaml
   ---
   title: "Your Post Title"
   date: 2026-01-15
   ---
   ```
3. Write your content below the front matter
4. Commit and push to deploy

## Obsidian Features

You can use wikilinks like this: [[another-post]]

You can also use wikilinks with display text: [[another-post|click here for another post]]

You can also embed HTML directly in your posts since `unsafe = true` is enabled.
